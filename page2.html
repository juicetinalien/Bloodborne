Fear the old blood.

Many travelers have journeyed to the city of Yharnam seeking a cure for their afflictions.

The Healing Church administered blood transfusions meant to cure those afflictions.

However that blood which cured their ailments turned them into fowl beasts.

The Hunter must overcome many of these beasts in order to find the source of the scourge.

The Healing Church was founded with the intention of exploring the Tomb of the Gods below Yharnam. There they found a holy medium deep within the tomb and with the knowledge they found, began to attempt healing their people with blood.
